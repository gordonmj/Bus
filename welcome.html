Welcome to my NJ Bus Time dashboard.

If you're like me* you might have several bus options in the morning, but not necessarily all at the same stop.

NJ Transit provides semi-accurate up-to-the-minute bus times, but it can be annoying to switch between pages for different stops, or wait for an SMS alert.

That's why I put this together. Currently, you can make your own "dashboard" with three stops on a single page.

More functionality to come later.

You can find buses from central Teaneck to New York here.

*Living in NJ and commuting via bus to Manhattan.
